ABOUT KY|TIES

KY|TIES is incorporating traditional Hmong embroidery and designs into a variety of ties. Our designs can be worn casually or at formal events with pride and are easily distinguishable as unique and cultural pieces.

FOUNDER


Welcome to my website. I am Ka Yeng Vue, founder of KY|TIES. I believe cultural identity is an important part of what makes us who we are in today’s world. KY | Ties’ story began when we were inspired to outfit men and women with classy, yet traditional Hmong-inspired reversible ties. Ka Yeng Vue, founder of KY | Ties, wanted men and women to have an affordable way to stay classy while dressed in authentic Hmong-inspired ties. In 2015, Vue forgot to bring one of two ties he had planned to wear to an event and that was when he realized, “hey, why don’t I make a two-sided tie?!” Each tie is handcrafted, then top-stitched together with a matching reverse side to create our unique Reversible Ties. (Don’t mind the top-stitching). We wanted to give our ties a unique touch of authentic Paj Ntaub (pa dao or Hmong inspired textile) while looking classy in today’s modern society. Here at KY | Ties, each tie is made with its own uniqueness and no two ties (or four - because they are double-sided) are ever the same. In the words of one of our customer and friend, “It’s something bout the way you feel when you hold the tie in your hands.” Once and always will be, authentic.
